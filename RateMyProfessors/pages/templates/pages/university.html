ABOUT US
“Silicon Valley Tbilisi” is the first private high-tech center that unites Business and Technology University, multi-million international employment platform, business incubator & accelerator, IT academy, media center, school, research center, VR laboratory, training center, pool and gyms in one space. Business and Technology University represents higher education and research unit that integrates business and modern digital technologies. Teaching, education and research activities are held by consolidating students, academic staff and researchers.
